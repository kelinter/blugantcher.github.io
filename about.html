Beyond the Page

When I'm not writing or editing, you can usually find me watching movies at the campus theatre, reading something new, or getting outside to climb, hike, or just move. Creative work is how I process the world, everything from a great film scene to a conversation with friends eventually finds its way into my stories and essays.

I love how creative communities make space for different voices and stories. That sense of connection and curiosity is at the heart of everything I write.

What's Next

I'm excited to keep growing as a writer, editor, and literary community member. In the next few years, I hope to continue working with literary magazines, pursue opportunities in publishing or journalism, and keep developing my own fiction and essays with a goal toward eventual publication.
