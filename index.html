Hello! I’m Blu Gantcher, a creative writing major at Bucknell University (’26) interested in fiction, literary journalism, and food writing. I love exploring how personal stories, place, and memory show up on the page.

I’ve participated in the Yale Writers’ Workshop, completed NYU programs in Op-Ed writing and food writing, and worked with the editorial team at West Branch, reading submissions and engaging with contemporary literature.

This site gathers a few of my favorite pieces—from short fiction to reported essays and literary journalism projects. Feel free to explore my work and reach out if you’d like to connect about writing, editing, or future opportunities.
